About me

I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in December 2023, supervised by Prof. Zhangjin Huang and Prof. Naijie Gu. Before that, I received the B.Sc. degree at the College of Information Engineering from Sichuan Agricultural University in 2018.

Research Interests: Object Pose Estimation, Human Activity Analysis, 3D Object Detection, Point Cloud Processing, etc.
