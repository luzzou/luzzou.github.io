About me

I am currently a third year Ph.D. student in University of Science and Technology of China, supervised by Prof. Zhangjin Huang and Prof. Naijie Gu. Before that, I received the B.Sc. degree at the College of Information Engineering from Sichuan Agricultural University in 2018.

Research Interests: Object Pose Estimation, Human Activity Analysis, 3D Object Detection, Point Cloud Processing, etc.
